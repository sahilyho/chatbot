Cherrie

Chatbot

The Cherrie Chatbot is a conversational interface that uses natural language processing and machine learning algorithms to understand and respond to user messages in a human-like way. The bot can answer a variety of questions, tell jokes, and provide weather and stock information upon request. The chatbot also has a personality, which is reflected in its responses and interactions with users. The goal of the project is to create a user-friendly and engaging chatbot that can help users with their queries and provide a pleasant conversational experience.
